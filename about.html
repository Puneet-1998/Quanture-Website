About Quanture Calanytics

An Institutional Fintech Built on Regulatory Integrity

Structured, compliant financial infrastructure services for regulated institutions and their business partners across India.

Company Background

Who We Are

India-incorporated fintech providing digital payment infrastructure across B2B and B2C — serving banks, business correspondents, and consumers through AEPS, DMT, BBPS, and CMS.

Fee-based revenue model. No public deposits, no speculative activities.

Vision & Mission

Purpose-Driven Financial Infrastructure

Our Vision

To be a trusted, compliance-led financial infrastructure provider enabling regulated institutions to extend accessible payment services to underserved segments of the Indian economy.

Our Mission

To deliver scalable, secure payment infrastructure to banks and business correspondents — enabling AEPS, DMT, BBPS, and cash management through a compliant platform that meets institutional standards.

Business Philosophy

Commitment to Compliance

Operational Standards That Meet Institutional Requirements

Regulatory Alignment
