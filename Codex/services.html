01 / 04 AEPS

Aadhaar-Enabled Payment System

AEPS workflows support biometric-assisted transactions through authorised channel partners.

  • Assisted transaction model for last-mile service access
  • Transaction logging and settlement reconciliation controls
  • Due diligence prerequisites for partner onboarding
  • Exception escalation pathways for service integrity
02 / 04 DMT

Domestic Money Transfer

DMT workflows enable authorised remittance facilitation through structured partner channels.

  • Domestic remittance processing with defined checkpoints
  • KYC and beneficiary validation requirements integrated into workflows
  • Transaction evidence generation for partner audit support
  • Risk and threshold monitoring aligned to compliance obligations
03 / 04 BBPS

Bharat Bill Payment System

BBPS-aligned bill payment service delivery supports compliant and traceable collection workflows.

  • Utility and category bill collection facilitation
  • Partner-facing transaction status and reconciliation checkpoints
  • Process design aligned with framework participation obligations
  • Documented operational governance for service continuity
04 / 04 CMS

Cash Management Services

Cash management workflows support institutional cash-handling requirements with reporting consistency and process controls.

  • Structured cash collection and route-level tracking
  • Reconciliation support for partner reporting and settlement cycles
  • Control checkpoints for exceptions and record completeness
  • Service documentation aligned to partner governance requirements
