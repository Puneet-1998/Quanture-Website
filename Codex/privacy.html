This Privacy Policy describes how Quanture Calanytics Private Limited handles information collected through institutional communication channels and related business interactions.

1. Scope

This policy applies to information collected through the website and institutional communication processes. The website is intended for authorised institutional and B2B audiences.

2. Information Categories

  • Business contact information provided by institutional representatives
  • Organisation profile and due diligence documentation for onboarding review
  • Operational data required for service delivery, monitoring, and reconciliation
  • Website interaction data necessary for basic site operation and security

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Institutional enquiry handling and partner onboarding workflows
  • Service delivery coordination and relationship management
  • Regulatory and compliance obligations, including AML and KYC controls where applicable
  • Audit readiness, governance reporting, and internal control management

4. Legal Basis

Information is processed on contractual necessity, legal obligation, legitimate institutional interest, and consent where specifically required.

5. Data Sharing

Information is not sold or traded. Data may be shared with authorised partners, service providers, and competent authorities where required by law or contractual scope.

6. Data Retention

Records are retained in line with applicable legal and regulatory obligations and documented internal retention schedules.

7. Information Security

Administrative, technical, and organisational controls are applied to protect information integrity and confidentiality. Controls include access governance and monitoring processes.

8. Data Subject Requests

Subject to applicable law and contractual constraints, requests for access, correction, or lawful deletion can be submitted through official channels.

The website may use limited functional cookies necessary for operation. No external analytics tooling is included in this implementation.
